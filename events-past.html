Midnight Dimsum

Miss the taste of authentic Chinese dim sum? Join Chinese Students Association at MIDNIGHT Dimsum for a twist on your favorite afternoon snack!

We will be serving umplings, spring rolls, fried rice, lo mein, egg tarts, custard buns, lotus buns, roast pork buns, sesame balls, shrimp dumpling, and shumai! Additionally there will be free performances from your favorite Cornell groups!

  • WHEN: Friday, November 15th 8PM-12AM
  • WHERE: Duffield Hall Atrium
  • PRICE: FREE!

Mid-Autumn Festival

Cornell Chinese Students Association welcome you to our 10th annual Mid-Autumn Festival on Ho Plaza! Come join us for one of the largest cultural events on campus, and enjoy a night of cultural performances, booths, and FREE FOOD! We'll be presenting a variety of Cornell's dance and music groups, as well as activities from several cultural organizations!

And throughout the night, we'll be providing FREE mooncakes and Chinese dishes for you to feast on! Cap off your homecoming with a bang, and invite your friends!

  • WHEN: Friday, October 4th 8PM-12AM
  • WHERE: Ho Plaza
  • PRICE: FREE!
