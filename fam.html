Family System

What is a fam?

  • a fam has Bigs (upperclassmen) and Littles (underclassmen)
  • form meaningful relationships and a support system!

Family Activities

Spending time with bigs/littles will earn your fam points! At the end of each semester, the fams with highest points get special prizes! You get points by posting pictures/videos of fam hangouts on the CSA Facebook page (note: at least 50% of fam are present with at least 1 big and 1 little):

  • family picture (5 points)
  • pictures at CSA events (5 points)
  • holidays or birthdays (5 points)
  • big/little treat (5 points per treat)
  • new double family hangout (5 points per new family)
  • videos (5 points each)
  • volunteering at Into The Streets (5 points for each member)
